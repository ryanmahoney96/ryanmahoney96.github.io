The One Dollar Promise

The Carthage Tiny Taxi company is a small business, by students, for students. Our goal is to take the stress out of getting to class, and to ensure students have access to safe travel around their campuses. Based out of Kenosha, Wisconsin, CTT was founded at Carthage College by Ryan Conrad Mahoney. Our vision is to spread the speedy and modern taxi service to campuses around the country, with a promise to never charge more than one dollar per trip.
